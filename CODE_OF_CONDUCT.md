# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Polar is a mental health tool. Everyone who uses it, builds it, or contributes to it deserves to do so in a safe and respectful environment.

We are committed to a community free from harassment, discrimination, and stigma — particularly around mental illness, substance use, and psychiatric treatment.

## Our standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Use welcoming, inclusive language
- Respect different experiences of mental illness — there is no single "right" experience
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Prioritize the wellbeing of users above technical preferences
- Handle all information about mental health with sensitivity and discretion

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Stigmatizing language about mental illness, psychiatric medication, or therapy
- Sharing or referencing personal mental health information without consent
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory comments of any kind
- Minimizing or dismissing people's mental health experiences
- Publishing others' private information

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other community spaces associated with Polar.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the maintainers via GitHub. All reports will be reviewed confidentially. Consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban from the project, depending on severity.
